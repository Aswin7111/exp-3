About Our Work

Founded in 2020, 3D Print Co. started as a small group of engineers with a vision to democratize advanced manufacturing. Today, we serve clients around the globe by providing innovative, efficient, and precise 3D printing and prototyping solutions.

We specialize in taking concepts from napkin sketches to finished products. Whether you're working on a science fair model, a startup product, or a mechanical part, we’re here to help bring your ideas to life.

Our Mission

To empower creators of all ages and backgrounds by providing accessible tools and expert support for turning imagination into physical reality.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Constantly exploring the newest 3D technologies and techniques.
  • Precision: We value high-quality, reliable outputs in every project.
  • Customer-first: We believe in listening, adapting, and delivering exactly what you need.
  • Sustainability: Supporting eco-friendly materials and minimal-waste processes.

Industries We Support

  • Education (schools, universities, makerspaces)
  • Healthcare (custom prosthetics, models for surgery)
  • Automotive (rapid design testing and spare part creation)
  • Consumer Goods (custom gadgets, toys, and cases)
  • Architecture (scale models and design visualizations)

We’re proud to be part of the growing global movement that is reshaping how things are designed and made. Join us in building the future—layer by layer.
